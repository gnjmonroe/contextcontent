Into the Post-Anthropocene

Prepare to be very sad. We have emitted greenhouse gases beyond the point of no return. The climate is doomed to change. Species are fated for extinction. The post-anthropocene is upon us. There is no reprieve for global society, and absolutely none to be found in this course. Donna Haraway, critical theorist and Professor at UC Santa Cruz writes, "there is a fine line between acknowledging the extent and seriousness of the troubles and succumbing to abstract futurism and its affects of sublime despair and its politics of sublime indifference." In this course, we will succumb to despair. Students can expect to develop skills in representing the magnitude of our environmental predicament, designing for scenarios with no agency, speculative resignation, and dank graphics.
