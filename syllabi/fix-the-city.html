Fix the city with this one weird trick

Read the entire brief. The answer will surprise you!

Experts have ruined the city. Modernist planning, avant garde design, best practices, what do that know? Our cities are dull and uninspired, nothing like they used to be and nothing that residents want!

This course will provide the one tool to resolve any and all urban problems. Students will rigorously study and precedents such as public meetings, night meetings, weekend meetings, charrettes, workshops, stickers, ancient forums and agora, Yelp, crowdsourcing the city, stickers, YouTube comments sections, and more. A series of invited speakers will provide lectures to the class. The final project is to put the one trick into practice and finally fix the city.
